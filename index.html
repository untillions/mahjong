Terms to Know

The game is Mahjong 麻将 (má jiāng: sounds like "maw-zhong").

Pronunciation

On this page you will see Chinese characters, Pinyin 拼音 (sounds like "pin-yin"), and "sounds like" english pronunciations. Pinyin (literally meaning "spelled sounds") uses the roman alphabet to spell out Chinese sounds. There are 4 different accents which represent the tone to be taken by the syllable. See below.

Char. Pinyin Tone Description
妈māflatflat, high-pitched
麻márisinglike ending a question
马mǎdippingfalling and then rising
骂màfallingstart high and fall abruptly
吗maneutrallight or no emphasis

The Suits

There are three suits (Characters, Bamboos, and Circles). Reading a tile in any of these suits is as simple as saying the number + the suit name together. Examples are shown below.

Characters Suit

萬 (wàn: sounds like "Juan")

In the characters suit, the character 萬 (wàn, meaning ten thousand) appears on the tile below each number.

Example: qī wàn ("chee Juan")

Bamboos Suit

索 (tiáo: sounds like "tsea-ow")

In the bamboos suit, the yī tile usually depicts a bird rather than a single bamboo stick and the bā tile usually is formed like a diamond, rectangle, or set of 'M's. The tiles can be distinguished by the number of bamboo sticks.

Example: èr tiáo ("er tsea-ow")

Circles Suit

筒子 (tǒng: sounds like "tong")

In the circles suit, all tiles can be distinguished by the number of circles.

Example: wǔ tǒng ("oo tong")

The Honors

There are two types of honor tiles: winds and dragons.

Wind Tiles

風牌 (fēngpái: sounds like "fung-pie")

Wind Char. Pinyin Sounds like
East東dōng"dong"
South南nán"naan" bread
West西xī"she"
North北běi"bay"

To name a wind tile, say the name of the wind and then the word for wind 風 (fēng: sounds like "fung").

Example: nán fēng ("naan fung")

Dragon Tiles

三元牌 (sānyuán pái: sounds like "sahn ywan pie")

Dragon Char. Pinyin Sounds like
Red紅中hóngzhōng"hong zhong"
Green發財fācái"fa ts-eye"
White白板báibǎn"bye bahn"

The red and green dragons have distinctive characters to mark the tile. The white dragon could be depicted as a blank tile, a black rectangle, or the character 白 (meaning "white"). To name a dragon tile, say its name.

Example: hóng zhōng ("hong zhong")

Bonus Tiles

There are two types of bonus tiles that operate in an identical way: flowers and seasons.
